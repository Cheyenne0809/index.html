File management is the act of organizing, storing, naming, retrieving, and protecting your digital files. This includes documents, photos, videos, music, and any other type of data stored electronically. Effective file management keeps your digital world organized and accessible, saving you time and frustration.

Fn today’s digital landscape, the ability to manage files and documents efficiently is essential for individuals and businesses alike. Sophisticated technology has transformed traditional storage methods into advanced file management systems

Managing space is another reason why is file management important. Keeping track of how much space your files take up allows you to delete unnecessary files and free up valuable storage.

A file management system has limited capabilities and is designed to manage individual or group files, such as special office documents and records. It may display report details, like owner, creation date, state of completion, and similar features useful in an office environment.
